PokéP Commands



Hello, welcome to the Commands page. To start, you can do '//help' or '//menu' on a Discord Server that has PokéP installed. All of the commands will come up. Some commands have to do with Pokémon, while others do not. PokéP is very self-explanatory and very easy to use. If you want to contact us for any sort of reason, please go to the Help/Support section up at the top.
